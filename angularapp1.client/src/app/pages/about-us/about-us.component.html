SnowBall Method Explained

Using our dashboard, we will proritise the account with the lowest balance. Continuing to make the minimum payments on the other accounts whilst attacking the lowest account.

When that one is paid off, delete it and move onto the next lowest card, moving what would have went to that card and putting it into the next one. The more accounts that are paid off, the more you can afford to pay off the next time.
